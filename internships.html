  • Accounting Career Awareness Program (ACAP) @ UC Berkeley

    Timeframe: July 10-15, 2016

    Paid: Unpaid

    Description

    A free one-week summer residency program designed to increase the numbers of underrepresented students of color pursuing careers in accounting and related business fields. Includes lectures, field trips and fun. Participants gain access to mentoring, internships, scholarships, and a social network.

    Requirements

    Open to students completing 9th -11th grade. Paper application with letter of recommendation received by 4/29/16.
